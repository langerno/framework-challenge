About Us

We are a small, family owned bakery in the heart of Lisbon, Portugal. Our family has owned this bakery since 1978, opening just a few years after Portugal's new constitution was established! Together, our entire family works to make delicious baked goods from delicious Pastel de Santa Clara to the iconic Pasatel de nata. We hope to provide the best possible baked goods to our customers and create a warm and welcoming atmosphere.
